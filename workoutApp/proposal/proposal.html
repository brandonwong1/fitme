Personalized Workout App

Problem Statement

Health and fitness is a subject that affect everyone. Fitness not only helps you live longer, it can help you live better. A fit body can lead to a healthier mind, and a healthier lifestyle. The wellbeing of a persons health can be largely correlated to their fitness levels. There are countless benefits to physical fitness such as a decreased risk of developing diseases, decreased risk of an injury, and mental health benefits (Curtis 2021). For these reasons, fitness is an aspect of life that we must consistently maintain in order to live happier lives.

However, getting to a desired fitnes level and maintaining it is not an easy feat. In addition, staying fit can be costly at times. From monthly gym memberships to purchasing a healthy diet along with the added protein powders, it may be overwhleming to breakthrough the friction of starting a fit lifestyle.

Many of the existing fitness applications and programs such as Noom comes at an expensive subscription fee that is focused on weight loss. However, fitness can also largely be subject to each individual. One individual may view fitness as losing weight while another may want to gain muscle mass and gain weight. The added subscription fee along with the price that comes with a fit lifestyle may be a hindrance to someone looking to find a solution to their fitness plan.

Users

Users of our app are people new to bodybuilding and in need of the proper information needed throughout their journey to reach their goals. Anyone can use the app, as one of the driving purposes for it is to create a positive, personalized experience for individual users. However, the recommended age to start bodybuilding is from late teens to early twenties, as the age is linked to time for muscle growth. We see the age to range of majority of the users accessing the website to be late teens to younger adults in their mid-late 20s.

Provided Information

Information users will view are details about recommended workout products, meal plans, and workout plans. For example, the app will give users recipes and nutritional contents in the meal plan, exercises will have descriptions on proper technique along with benefits/specific muscle groups they target.

User App Interaction

Users will be able to take a personalized quiz in order to tailor the website to their needs. Quizzes taken can help users find personalized workout plans, meal plans, and recommended workout products. Users will also be able to input goals they would like to achieve and use it as a performance tracker. If users aren’t satisfied with the results of the quiz, there will be another page where they can see the extensive list of workout plans, meal plans, and workout products. They’ll be able to search for specific products, workouts, and meal plans as well as use filters to get desired results.

Addressing the Problem

By using our app, users will have confidence to workout, which lowers the current barriers that exist for new bodybuilders. Many workout websites currently are generalized and don’t have content that meets users’ needs exactly so our app will allow for users to see more meaningful content. People go into working out for many reasons and not just body building, so personalizing the site will allow users to relate more with the content being shown and possibly keep them more motivated to follow through with their goals.
